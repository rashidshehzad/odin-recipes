Ingredients

Make sure to have the following ingredients on hand before starting.

  • Italian sausage and lean ground beef
  • Onion and garlic
  • 1 can of crushed tomatoes, 2 cans of tomato sauce and 2 cans of tomato paste
  • 2 tablespoons of white sugar
  • Fresh parsley, dried basil leaves, salt, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds and black pepper
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Parmesan, mozzarella and ricotta cheese
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna .

    5. How to layer lasagna

    1. Meat sauce
    2. Noodles
    3. Ricotta mixture
    4. Mozzarella slices
    5. Meat sauce
    6. Parmesan cheese
    7. Repeat the layers, then top with the remaining parmesan
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
