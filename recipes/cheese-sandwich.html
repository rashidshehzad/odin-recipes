Cheese sandwich

The following instructions were written in a minute by me.

Description

A cheese sandwich is a sandwich with cheese.

Everyone knows how to make one.

Ingredients

Make sure you have the following ingredients but you can use anything really.

  • 2 slices of Bread
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Put a slice of bread down somewhere.
  2. Put cheese on the bread
  3. Put the second slice of bread on top.

Voila, you are now done.
